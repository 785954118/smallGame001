短：老少皆宜的休闲益智类游戏。
Leisure puzzle games suitable for all ages.
长：一款老少皆宜的益智游戏，目的是在训练你的逻辑思考能力。要求把木箱放到指定的位置，稍不小心就会出现箱子无法移动或者通道被堵住的情况，所以需要巧妙的利用有限的空间和通道，合理安排移动的次序和位置，才能顺利的完成任务。还可以选择计时模式来挑战自己的反应和操作速度的上限，看一下你是否能用最少的时间和步数来完成全部关卡的挑战！
A puzzle game suitable for all ages, the purpose is to train your logical thinking ability. It is required to put the wooden box in the designated position. If you are not careful, the box will not move or the passage will be blocked. Therefore, it is necessary to skillfully use the limited space and passage and arrange the moving order and position reasonably in order to successfully complete the task. You can also choose the timing mode to challenge the upper limit of your reaction and operation speed, and see if you can complete all the challenges in the least time and steps!
